# Project Summary: The Fremont Blueprint

### Vision
To establish the global standard for verifiable, responsible, and human-centric AI through the first municipal implementation of the Signet Protocol.

### Key Deliverables
* **Municipal Integrity Dashboard:** A live portal for residents of Fremont, CA to verify city-issued AI communications.
* **Master Signatory Framework:** A standardized method for officials to take legal and ethical "Authority" over AI outputs.
* **Scale-Up Toolkit:** Procurement templates and MOU frameworks to facilitate adoption by 50+ cities by 2027.

### Impact
Signet bridges the gap between frontier AI research and municipal accountability, making AI transparency a tangible civic right for 230,000 residents.
